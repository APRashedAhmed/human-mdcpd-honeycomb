In this experiment you will watch videos of a ball bouncing around the screen that changes colors between red, green, and blue. At the end of each video, a new screen will be shown where you will be asked to report the final color of the ball by pressing 1, 2, or 3 on your keyboard.

Shortly after you make your color selection, the next video will begin, repeating the process. Videos and their corresponding choice-screens will be presented as quickly as possible to minimize the time it takes to complete the full experiment.
