In this experiment you will watch videos of a ball bouncing around the screen that changes colors between red, green, and blue. At the end of each video, a new screen will be shown where you will be asked to report the final color of the ball by clicking on the buttons for red, green, or blue . You will then be asked to enter how confident you were in your response with a slider

Shortly after you adjust the confidence slider, the next video will begin, repeating the process. It's expected that you enter a choice and indicate your confidence to the best of your ability on each video. Videos and their corresponding choice-screens will be presented as quickly as possible to minimize the time it takes to complete the full experiment.
