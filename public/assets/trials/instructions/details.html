In this experiment you will watch videos of a ball bouncing around the screen that changes colors between red, green, and blue.

At the end of each video, a new screen will be shown where you will be asked to guess the final color of the ball and how confident you are in your response. Shortly after, the next video will begin, repeating the process.


When we calculate the final score for the task, the confidence you select on a trial behaves like a wager, where a higher confidence on a correct guess gives you more points, but also takes away more points if you are incorrect.


You will have 5 seconds to input a color and confidence, and will lose points if you do not provide an input in time.
