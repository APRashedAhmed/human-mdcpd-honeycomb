As mentioned earlier, the ball will move around the screen and change colors between red, green, and blue, in that order ( R-> G-> B-> R-> G-> B...).

Once the video ends, you will be shown a screen where you must select what you think the final color of the ball is, and how confident you are. In this video, you can directly see the final color of the ball is green.

Click next to view the color choice and confidence screens, and please select green and high confidence.
