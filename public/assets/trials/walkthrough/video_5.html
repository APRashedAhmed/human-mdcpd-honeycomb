The real task will have a gray filter in the middle of the screen which allows you to see the position of the ball but hides the color when it passes through. Importantly, the ball can change color when passing through the gray filter. In this video, the ball ends as red in the visible region, but for most videos the ball will end behind the screen.

Once the video ends, click next to view the color choice and confidence screens, and please select red and high confidence.
