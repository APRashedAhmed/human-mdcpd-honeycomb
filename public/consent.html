Consent for Research Participation

You have been invited to take part in a research study on decision making that is being conducted at Brown University. Your participation is voluntary.

Purpose of the Research

The purpose of the study is to investigate how people understand multiple visual features that dynamically change.

Inclusion Criteria

You must be at least 18 years old to participate in this experiment, and you must be a native English speaker.

Behavioral Procedures

The task involves watching videos of a ball bouncing around the screen as it changes color and pressing on the keyboard to indicate the final color of the ball.

Time Required

The full task will require approximately one hour to complete.

Compensation

You will receive TOTAL_COMPENSATION for your time.

Risks

You might get tired while doing the task or have some discomfort from sitting still and minimizing your movement. The task will include breaks between each block at which time you will have the opportunity to stretch and move as necessary.

Benefits

There are no direct benefits to participating in this study.

Confidentiality

Your participation in this study is confidential. By agreeing to this document, you permit us to use anonymized information for scientific purposes, including teaching and/or publication.

Withdrawal

You are free to withdraw at any time during the course of the study by closing the browser.

Researchers
